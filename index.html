About Us

The Story Behind
Pure Veg Kitchen

Founded by Gayatri Shukla in the heart of Kalyanpur, Lucknow, Pure Veg Kitchen is born from a passion for authentic homemade vegetarian cuisine and a commitment to health and purity.

Every dish is prepared with the same love and care as cooking for family. We source fresh ingredients daily, follow traditional recipes passed down through generations, and maintain the highest standards of hygiene in our cloud kitchen.
